Stella Di Mare Sea Club Hotel

This property is 5 minutes walk from the beach. Located next to Ain Soukhna’s port overlooking the Red Sea, this hotel offers an outdoor pool, spa and gym. Each air-conditioned room includes a balcony or terrace and satellite TV. All Stella Di Mare Sea Club Hotel’s rooms are housed in villas with each villa containing 6 rooms. Every villa has its own private swimming pool. Some rooms have a seating area. Many activities are available at Stella Di Mare Sea Club Hotel, including diving, canoeing and mini golf. Guests can relax with a massage, in the sauna or with a Turkish bath. A game of tennis or squash can be enjoyed on the court. Nile Oasis restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine overlooking the pool. Asia Restaurant offers Far East dishes, the best of Szechuan and Mandarin cuisine.There is also a pool bar and a beach bar serving refreshing drinks and food including homemade pizza and pasta. At Café Moresco, traditional gourmet coffee, tea and snacks are served. Free private parking is possible at Hotel Stella Di Mare Sea Club. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.0 for a two-person trip.
