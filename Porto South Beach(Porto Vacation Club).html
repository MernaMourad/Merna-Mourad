Porto South Beach(Porto Vacation Club)

Boasting a private beach area, Porto South Beach (Porto Vacation Club) is set in Ain Sokhna. Featuring a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, this 4-star resort has air-conditioned rooms with a private bathroom. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk and a cash machine for guests. The units in the resort are equipped with a TV. Porto South Beach (Porto Vacation C provides some units with sea views, and all rooms come with a balcony. All guest rooms will provide guests with bed linen and towels. A buffet breakfast is available daily at the accommodation. Porto South Beach (Porto Vacation Club) offers a terrace. Ras Sedr is 28 km from the resort.
