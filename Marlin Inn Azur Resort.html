Marlin Inn Azur Resort

This property is 1 minute walk from the beach. Situated in the Al Mamsha El Seyahi district of Hurghada, Marlin Inn Azur Resort offers a private beach area, barbecue facilities and a bar. The property is close to several well-known attractions, 14 km from Coast Line, 16 km from Malek Disha Dive Spot and 22 km from Abu Hashish Dive Spot. The resort boasts an outdoor swimming pool, evening entertainment and a 24-hour front desk. At the resort every room is fitted with a balcony. Marlin Inn Azur Resort features certain rooms with sea views, and rooms include a private bathroom. All units in the accommodation are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a hairdryer. A buffet breakfast is available daily at Marlin Inn Azur Resort. You can play table tennis at this 4-star resort. Subaqua Dive Centre Sunrise is 23 km from the resort, while Jetty Makadi Bay is 23 km away. The nearest airport is Hurghada International Airport, 4 km from Marlin Inn Azur Resort.
