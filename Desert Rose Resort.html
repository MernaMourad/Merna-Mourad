Desert Rose Resort

This 5-star resort is located in the heart of Hurghada and offers air-conditioned rooms with a private balcony. It includes 3 swimming pools, a spa, a children's pool and a private beach along the Red Sea. It also includes an aqua park. Desert Rose’s spacious rooms include a satellite TV and a minibar. Some come with a separate seating area. The bathrooms are stocked with toiletries and a hairdryer. Relaxing massage treatments are offered at Planet Beach Spa Centre including a gym and a sauna. Entertainment is provided for children at the kids club. Windsurfing and snorkelling are possible at the beach. Located on the beach, II Delfino restaurant serves freshly prepared seafood. Italian cuisine is served at Dolce Vita, while the Palm main restaurant serves buffet dishes. Fattoosh offers Lebanese dishes. The property has 9 bars including Tropicana Pool Bar, Bella Vista and Ali Baba serving Oriental drinks and shisha. Hurghada International Airport is 10 minutes’ drive away, and a limo service is available. Free WiFi is offered in public areas. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.6 for a two-person trip. This property is also rated for the best value in Hurghada! Guests are getting more for their money when compared to other properties in this city.
