Welcome To Alexandria Hotels

Tolip Hotel Alexandria Tolip Hotel Alexandria offers accommodations in Alexandria. The hotel has a seasonal outdoor pool and playground, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.


Royal Crown Hotel Located in Alexandria, 0.6 miles from Al-Salam Theater, Royal Crown Hotel features a restaurant and free WiFi. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. All rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV.


Paradise Inn Beach Resort Overlooking Mamoura beach, Paradise Inn Beach Resort offers rooms with a spa bathtub and a balcony. The hotel has a large pool and spacious gardens.


Semiramis Hotel Semiramis is a beachfront hotel in downtown Alexandria. It has a restaurant and free Wi-Fi in public areas. Alexandria Zoo is 2.5 miles away.


Aifu Resort - El Montazah Showcasing an outdoor pool, seasonal outdoor pool and terrace, Aifu Resort - El Montazah is located in Alexandria. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Free private parking is available on site.
