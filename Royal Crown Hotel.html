Royal Crown Hotel

This property is 12 minutes walk from the beach. Set in Alexandria, within a 2.3 km radius of Alexandria Citadel and Alexandria Zoo, Royal Crown Hotel offers accommodation with a shared lounge and a restaurant, and free WiFi. Built in 1989, this hotel is within an 11-minute walk of Catacombs of Kom el Shoqafa and Al-Salam Theatre. Attractions in the area include Alexandria Sporting Club, 1 km away, or Smouha Sporting Club, situated 1.5 km from the property. All units at the hotel are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. The units at Royal Crown Hotel include air conditioning and a wardrobe. A buffet breakfast is served daily at the property. Staff at the 24-hour front desk speak Arabic and English. Stanley Bridge is 1.7 km from the accommodation.
