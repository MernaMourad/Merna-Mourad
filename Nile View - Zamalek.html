Nile View - Zamalek

Boasting on-site dining and free WiFi, Nile View - Zamalek is set in Cairo, 500 m from New Cairo Opera and 600 m from El-Ahly Sporting Club. Well located in the Gezira district, the property is located a 14-minute walk from Sudan Embassy and 1.3 km from Nile Hilton Shopping Centre. The property features river views, an outdoor pool and a 24-hour front desk. All rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Rooms have a private bathroom with free toiletries. All units at Nile View - Zamalek feature air conditioning and a desk. The accommodation offers an American or continental breakfast. Nile View - Zamalek offers a children's playground. Ag Museum is 1.4 km from the hotel, while Cairo Museum is 1.4 km from the property. Cairo International Airport is 19 km away.
