Cancun Sokhna Resort Managed by Accorhotels

Located directly on the Red Sea, the resort offers a holiday experience that caters perfectly for all tastes. Guests can enjoy the beach, pool or the variety of activities offered, while children have their own fun at the kids' club. Five restaurants and bars offer a range of dining options, and the 24-hour room service provides delicious meals to enjoy while relaxing in your room. Private balconies and flat-screen TVs are available in all rooms at the Cancun Resort. Each stylish room has satellite TV and a tea/coffee maker. Some overlook the Red Sea. The Cancun Resort serves a large buffet breakfast. It also offers local specialities at its elegant restaurant. Facilities include a gym, massage services and an outdoor swimming pool. Free private parking is available. This property is also rated for the best value in Ain Sokhna! Guests are getting more for their money when compared to other properties in this city.
