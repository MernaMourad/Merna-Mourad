Sofitel Cairo Nile El Gezirah

Sofitel El Gezirah is a 5-star luxury hotel with a private promenade along the Nile. It is within a walking distance from the Opera house and Cairo Tower, as well as Egyptian Museum and Khan El Khalili. It features infinity outdoor pool. Free WiFi is available in all areas. All guest rooms at the Sofitel El Gezirah hotel feature air conditioning, satellite TV, free basic WiFi and minibar. Many rooms provide far-reaching views along the River Nile and across the city of Cairo. The hotel has 7 restaurants, which include Moroccan, Egyptian and international specialties. There are also 5 bars including the trendy Ana's bar and the library tea lounge. Sofitel El Gezirah Cairo has a range of leisure facilities, including a spa complete with a swimming pool, a hammam, and fitness centre. A hot tub is available with massage packages. Tours and trips can be arranged at the concierge desk. Sofitel El Gezirah is 20 km from the international airport. Private on site car parking is available. This property also has one of the best-rated locations in Cairo! Guests are happier about it compared to other properties in the area.
